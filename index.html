Hi, I'm Joyce.

I study Statistics & Philosophy at Harvard with a secondary in CS.

I don't have a grand thesis about life (yet?), but I care a lot about

  • using data in compelling & responsible ways,
  • solving hard problems, &
  • storytelling as a method of bridging gaps.

I hope I can do all these things in the future.

Outside of class, I conduct youth polling with the Harvard Public Opinion Project & mentor neighborhood kids at the Harvard Ed Portal in Allston.

In my free time, I like to translate movies from Chinese to English. I am also an editor for Sine Theta, an international creative arts magazine featuring artists of the Sino diaspora. Some of my favorite things include the ProPublica Data Store, the Dense Discovery newsletter, & the Knowing Better YouTube channel.

This site exists mostly because I wanted to pick up some web dev skills.
